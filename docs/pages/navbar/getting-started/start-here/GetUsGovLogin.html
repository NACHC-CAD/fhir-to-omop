Get A Login for US Government Accounts

Some of the terminologies used by OMOP require a UMLS account. To get a UMLS account follow the steps below.

Submit A Request for A login.gov Account

Submit a request for a login.gov account by going to https://secure.login.gov/.

On the following screens enter your email, language choice, accept the terms, etc.
You will be sent an email. Follow the instructions in the email to finish the process.
